What is a fractional Chief Revenue Officer?

A fractional Chief Revenue Officer is an experienced revenue leader who provides strategic guidance and leadership on a part-time, short-term, or long-term basis to small and medium-sized businesses that may not have the resources to hire a full-time executive.

How can Aesop help improve customer conversion rates?

Aesop uses data-driven insights and creative strategies tailored to your specific business needs to enhance sales conversion rates, drive growth, and increase revenue.

What types of companies can benefit from Aesop’s services?

Aesop serves small to medium-sized businesses, midsized companies, SaaS teams, studios, and publishers looking to improve sales, sales operations, customer support, marketing, retention, and revenue growth.

What are the financial benefits of hiring a fractional CRO instead of a full-time executive?

Companies gain access to top-tier talent and strategic leadership without the financial burden of a full-time executive salary, allowing teams to allocate resources more efficiently while still focusing on growth.
