The post-launch cliff

The problem is not the game. It is the system around it.

Every studio knows the rush of launch day: the flood of players, coverage, and community buzz. But by month four, many teams see the same sharp drop-off: fewer players, flat revenue, and quiet channels.

That collapse is not random. It is predictable and preventable. Aesop helps studios design systems that keep games alive post-launch, and helps publishers apply those principles across a portfolio.

Recovery Framework

The framework behind sustainable growth.

The studios that thrive treat growth like design: deliberate, tested, and repeatable. Aesop aligns creative, business, and community loops into one rhythm.

Light Systems

15-minute weekly sync across key functions, a shared dashboard everyone can read, clear owners for each initiative, and no status meetings, just coordination.

Player and Partner Loop

Plan content drops that give players reasons to return, activate community events and creator programs, and use partnerships and bundles to expand reach.

Predictable Runway

Forecast cash windows around real creative milestones, align content cadence with revenue rhythm, and use lightweight metrics to guide timing without slowing creativity.
